About Us

About SERA Bangladesh

Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Dhaka, SERA Bangladesh is a leading consultancy dedicated to fostering sustainable and ecological economies throughout Bangladesh. Our strategic location allows us to facilitate solution-oriented initiatives across rural regions, metropolitan cities, and beyond.

We leverage our comprehensive expertise and experience to develop customized sustainable and ecological concepts and strategies for a wide range of projects. From project management and government consulting to value chain development and environmental sustainability implementation, SERA Bangladesh offers a tailored suite of services to meet your specific needs.

This commitment to sustainable development extends to our partnerships with businesses, governments, and organizations. We deliver innovative solutions, facilitate impactful collaborations, and support initiatives that drive positive change. Our comprehensive service portfolio encompasses project management, sector-specific consulting, non-profit support, educational programs, technology solutions, market entry guidance, organic certification, agricultural research, and development fund mediation.

At SERA Bangladesh, we are passionate about building resilient futures. We empower communities, protect the environment, and promote equitable development to advance sustainable economies that benefit all.

Meet the Team

SERA Bangladesh is an organization deeply committed to the belief that its people are the cornerstone of its success, a principle that has consistently been validated. The team members are exceptionally talented individually, yet it is the synergy of these talents, aligned with the company's vision, that distinguishes the workplace as exceptional.

The SERA Bangladesh team includes a skilled and collaborative group of highly capable members, all united by the shared goal of delivering top-quality services. The organization prioritizes creating a workplace that is both enjoyable and welcoming while also fostering personal and career advancement.

The success of SERA Bangladesh is rooted in adherence to the core values that drive the organization. These values support collaboration and foster an inspiring and stimulating work environment. SERA Bangladesh takes immense pride in the dedication and exemplary performance of its team and is confident in its hardworking and creatively intelligent team to lead SERA Bangladesh to an even brighter future.
