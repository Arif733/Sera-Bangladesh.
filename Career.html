Communication Intern (Female)

Job Description:

As a Communication Intern, you will support the communication team in developing and implementing communication strategies. You will assist in creating content, managing social media, and coordinating internal and external communication efforts.

Education:

  • Bachelor’s degree in English, Mass Communication, Journalism and related subjects from any reputed university.

Additional Requirements:

  • Age 22 to 28 years.
  • Female candidates are eligible to apply.
  • Basic ideas in writing press releases, reports, social media articles, and web blogs.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills to effectively convey information and ideas.
  • Basic understanding and ability to use design tools like Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Illustrator, and Canva to create visual content (graphics, videos, infographics).

Working Environment:

  • Working Days and Hours: Sunday to Thursday (excluding public holidays)
  • PM Remuneration: Paid
  • Duration: 06 Months initially
  • Food: Lunch (Office Provided)
  • Job Location: Dhaka

Responsibilities:

  • Develop clear, engaging, and informative written, visual, and digital content (documentation, media relations materials, newsletters, blog posts, social media content, graphics, videos, and infographics) to support organizational goals and communication strategies.
  • Assist in the development and implementation of effective communication strategies to achieve organizational goals.
  • Communicate effectively with team members and stakeholders to ensure alignment and understanding.
  • Be prepared to undertake additional tasks as assigned by management.

Project Management Intern (Female)

Job Description:

As a Project Assistant Intern, you will support the project management team in planning, executing, and finalizing projects according to strict deadlines. You will assist with the coordination of resources, and will liaise with team members and stakeholders to ensure project objectives are met.

Education:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Economics, Social Science, anthropology, Business Administration from any reputed university.

Additional Requirements:

  • Age 22 to 28 years
  • Organizational and multitasking skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • The ability to collaborate effectively with colleagues towards achieving common goals.
  • Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to identify and resolve issues independently.

Working Environment:

  • Working Days and Hours: Sunday to Thursday (excluding public holidays)
  • PM Remuneration: Paid
  • Duration: 06 Months initially
  • Food: Lunch (Office Provided)
  • Job Location: Dhaka

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in planning, tracking, and reporting on project progress. Coordinate activities, resources, and information flow to ensure smooth project execution.
  • Help prepare project documentation, presentations, and reports, effectively communicating project objectives and results.
  • Conduct thorough research and gather information necessary to complete project tasks effectively.
  • Support the project manager with administrative tasks, ensuring project efficiency and organization.
  • Maintain comprehensive documentation, plans, and reports to facilitate project transparency and future reference.
